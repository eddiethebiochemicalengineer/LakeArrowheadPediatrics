Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. If you can’t find what you need, please call our office.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We do our best to accommodate walk-ins when possible. Calling ahead is the best way to ensure we can see you promptly.

Do you offer same-day sick visits?

Often, yes. Please call as early as possible and we’ll work with you to find the best option.

What should I bring to our first visit?

Insurance card, parent/guardian ID, any immunization records you have, and completed new patient forms.

How do after-hours calls work?

Call our main number for guidance. For emergencies (trouble breathing, severe injury, etc.), call 911.

Do you have a patient portal?

Yes—use the Patient Portal page for login and bill pay links (as available).
