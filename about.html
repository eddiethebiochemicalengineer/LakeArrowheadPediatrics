About Lake Arrowhead Pediatrics

For more than 20 years, Dr. Martin Rocha and our team have cared for children and teens across the Lake Arrowhead community—offering attentive visits, practical guidance, and a warm clinic experience for families.

Our approach

We listen first. We take time to understand what’s going on and what matters most to your family.

We explain options clearly. You’ll leave with a plan you understand—plus follow‑up guidance when needed.

We care for the whole child. From preventive care to chronic conditions and behavioral concerns, we support growth, learning, and wellbeing.

Clinic setting: We’re proud to serve families in the San Bernardino Mountains and the greater Lake Arrowhead area.

Safety & infection control

We follow standard medical clinic infection control practices, including routine cleaning and guidance for sick visits. If you think your child may have a contagious illness, please call ahead so we can advise you on the best next step.
